FULL STACK DEVELOPER!!

Full stack web developers have the ability to design complete web applications and websites. They work on the frontend, backend, database and debugging of web applications or websites. Full stack developers have a comprehensive understanding of the entire web application, from the front end to the back end. This holistic knowledge allows them to take complete ownership of the project, making informed decisions and ensuring a cohesive development process. They can anticipate potential challenges, optimize performance, and create seamless integrations between different components of the application.
